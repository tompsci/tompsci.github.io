Dissertation: Generating Movie Reviews

An exploration of methods for generating prose about movies. Using various methods and sources of data about film - descriptions, reviews and metadata, I attempted to create texts about films using Markov chains, templated bodies of text, NLG methodology and sentiment analysis. I created a small wordpress-style blog website and a twitter bot to attempt to foster some outside interraction with the generated works and see how people would respond.
